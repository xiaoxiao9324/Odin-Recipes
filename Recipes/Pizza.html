Donatello's Pizza

Description

  • Pizza dough
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni Slices
  • Traditonal Pizza Sauce
  • Garlic Butter

Step By Step Instructions

  1. Form the dough into a disc
  2. Spread the sauce all around the dough leaving a dry edge for the crust
  3. Sprinkle cheese all over the sauce liberally
  4. Lay the pepperoni slices all round the cheese in a round pattern
  5. Brush the garlic butter all around the crust
  6. bake at 325 for 15 minutes
