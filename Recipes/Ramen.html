Chicken Flavored Ramen Noodles

Description

  • Package of Maruchan Chicken Flavored Ramen
  • Stove and Pot
  • Water

Step By Step Instructions

  1. Open all packages
  2. Fill pot with 3 cups of water and heat on high until boiling
  3. PLace block of ramen into the boiling water for 2-3 minutes or till preferred Al Dente
  4. Turn off heat and stir in the included flavor packet
  5. Drain out excess stock if wanted and let cool for 1 minute
