Classic PB & J Sandwich

Description

  • Sliced Bread
  • Peanut Butter (Smooth or Crunchy)
  • Preffered Flavor of Jelly

Step By Step Instructions

  1. Open all packages
  2. Spread the Peanut Butter on one slice of bread
  3. Spread the Jelly onto the other slice of bread
  4. Slap those bad boys together and you've got your sandwich
